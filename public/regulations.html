Regulations

Please read and follow the regulations carefully.

General Rules

  • All participants must register through the official registration form.
  • Participants must adhere to the tournament schedule.
  • Respect all players and tournament staff.

Code of Conduct

  • No cheating or use of unauthorized software.
  • Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • Maintain a professional and respectful demeanor at all times.

Match Rules

  • All matches will be played on the designated game server.
  • Match results must be reported within 15 minutes of completion.
  • In case of disputes, the decision of the tournament officials is final.
